# Mastering the compose box
<span style="color:black;font-weight:100;font-size:13px">
When composing a message, Zulip lets you view a different conversation from the one you are composing to. For example, you can start a new topic without changing your narrow, send a private message about the topic you're viewing, or look up a related discussion.
</span>
</br>
<span style="color:black;font-weight:100;font-size:13px;">
In this context, the parts of the message view that are outside of the conversation you are composing to are faded for clarity.
</span>

## Go to conversation
<span style="color:black;font-weight:100;font-size:13px;">
Zulip allows you to limit the message view to the conversation to which you are currently composing.
</span>

!!! tip ""
   
    1. Open the [compose box](https://zulip.com/help/open-the-compose-box).
    &nbsp; 
    2. If composing a stream message, click the '**Go to conversation**'  button in the compose box.
    
    **KeyBoard Shortcut:**  For bothe stream and private messages, use 
    <kbd>Crtl</kbd> + <kbd>.</kbd> to go to the conversation you are composing.

#
### Toggle between <kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>Enter</kbd> and <kbd>Enter</kbd> to send a message
<span style="color:black;font-weight:100;font-size:13px">
By default, the <kbd>Enter</kbd> key adds a new line to your message,
and <kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>Enter</kbd> sends your message.
</span></br>

<span style="color:black;font-weight:100;font-size:13px">
    This is convenient for typing multi-line messages, which are more common in Zulip than in most other chat products. However, you can also configure Zulip so that the <kbd>Enter</kbd> key sends your message.
</span>


!!! tip ""

    **Tip:** <kbd>Shift</kbd> + <kbd>Enter</kbd> always adds a new line, regardless of whether
    
    **<kbd>Enter</kbd> to send** is enabled.

#
### Enable <kbd>Enter</kbd> to send

!!! tip ""
   
      1. Open the [compose box](https://zulip.com/help/open-the-compose-box).
      &nbsp; 
      2. Click **<kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>Enter</kbd>** to send in the bottom right corner of the compose box , just below the **Send** button. 
      &nbsp;
      3. Select **<kbd>Enter</kbd>** to send. 



#
### Enable **<kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>Enter</kbd> to send**

!!! tip ""

    1. Open the [compose box](https://zulip.com/help/open-the-compose-box)
    &nbsp; 
    2. Click **<kbd>Enter</kbd>** to send in the bottom right corner of the compose box, just below the Send button
    &nbsp;
    3. Select **<kbd>Ctrl</kbd> + <kbd>Enter</kbd>** to send
