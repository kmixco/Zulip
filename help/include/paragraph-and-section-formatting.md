## Paragraphs and sections

Here is an example of how you can create paragraphs and add a horizontal rule.

### What you type

```
One blank space for a new paragraph
New line, same paragraph

New paragraph

---, ***, or ___ for a horizontal line
Over the line

---

Under the line
```
